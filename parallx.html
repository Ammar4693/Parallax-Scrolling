Nissan GT-R

The Nissan GT-R, also known as the "Godzilla," is a high-performance sports car renowned for its exceptional speed and handling capabilities. It boasts mind-blowing acceleration, thanks to its powerful engine and advanced all-wheel-drive system.

Toyota Supra MK4

The Supra MK4 is powered by the legendary 2JZ-GTE engine, a 3.0-liter inline-six with twin turbochargers. This engine is known for its robust construction, smooth power delivery, and potential for significant horsepower gains with modifications.

The Classic Ambassador

The Ambassador is an iconic car that holds a special place in the history of the Indian automotive industry. It was manufactured by Hindustan Motors in India and became a symbol of status, power, and government representation.
